Welcome to the official web page of Sugar Land Munchies. This is the brand new cafeteria for UH Sugar Land students to use their meal plans at when on the Sugar Land Campus. We are so excited for you to come in and try our amazing food. Our mission is to lower food expenses for students so that when they are at Sugar Land for class, they can use their meal plan, rather than spending more money to buy food.

Please feel free to explore our web page, you will find information on our daily menu for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, along with nutritional information. You can also learn about owner, Zachery D'Andrea, and read our weekly blog that updates each week offering students friendly and valuable advice.



We are open from 08:00 AM to 09:00 PM on Mondays thru Thursday and from 08:00 AM to 03:00 PM on Fridays. We are closed on the weekends. Please come by when your at University of Houston - Sugar Land.
